Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Vanilla Ice Cream in a cone

Ingerdienta

  • Milk
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla

Step by step guide

  1. Cook the milk
  2. Add Vanilla extract
  3. Let it rest over night
  4. Pour the mix into an ice cream maker
  5. Serve immediately or ripen in the freezer before serving
